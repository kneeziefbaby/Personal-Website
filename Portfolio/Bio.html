    Brief Background

    Hi everyone! My name is Kai and I am an "Airforce Brat" who moved to Colorado in the Winter of 2011 from Southern California. Colorado is officially home now and I guess one could say I have embraced everything the state has to offer without looking back! This includes learning about and exploring a huge IT and tech community.

    Academic Interests

    I am currently a student at Colorado State University with a graduation date of Spring '24. I initially went to school for engineering, but found it not to be a natural fit for me. With time off from school I have developed a hunger to aspire for more and develop a career that I can be proud of.The Covid-19 pandemic brought me the perfect opportunity to reevaluate life and choose a promising career. My analytical and inquisitive mindset, coupled with my love for computers and everything about them has lead me to choosing a career in computer information systems (CIS) specializing in cybersecurity. For me, it is not just important to know how systems and computers work, I want to know how to safeguard networks and protect everyone from malicious attackers.
